The AOS_AOF.pt model was (painstakingly, on free Google Colab GPUs) trained for 12650 epochs on the images from the games Age of Strategy (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.zts.ageofstrategy) and Age of Fantasy (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.zts.ageoffantasy), as well as the art from this website (http://pousse.rapiere.free.fr/tome/tiles/AngbandTk/tome-angbandtkmonsterstiles.htm)
